We are proud students of the Information Systems (IS) course at the Faculty of Computing, Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka. Our faculty offers a range of cutting-edge programs, catering to diverse fields in computing and technology. The IS course focuses on bridging the gap between technology and business, equipping us with the skills to , implement, and manage innovative information systems that solve real-world problems. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical applications, we are being prepared to excel in roles like system analysts, IT consultants, and business technology leaders, making a meaningful impact in the ever-evolving digital world.

Colombo meetup


Our first meetup in April 2024 at Viharamahadevi Park was a unique experience, as we hadn't even started our university lectures yet. We were all freshers, excited and curious to meet the people who would become our batchmates. The day was full of introductions and icebreaker games that helped us get comfortable with each other. Laughter and friendly competition brought us closer as we played games that let us show off our skills and sense of humor. This initial gathering set a positive tone for our friendships, giving us a sense of belonging before we even began our studies.

Kandy meetup


In August, after a few months of online classes, we met again at Peradeniya Garden in Kandy. By then, we knew each other a bit better, and the reunion felt like meeting old friends. We continued the tradition of playing games, enjoying activities that encouraged teamwork and gave us a break from our studies. With the stunning garden as our backdrop, we spent the day sharing stories, playing games, and taking in the peaceful surroundings. This meetup strengthened our bonds even further, making us feel like a tight-knit group ready to support each other through our university journey.
