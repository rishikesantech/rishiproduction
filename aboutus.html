Who we are

Welcome to Rishikesan.Tech!

We are a group of five friends from Jaffna, united by the shared memories of our first-year university days at Sabaragamuwa University. What started as a simple bond of friendship has now blossomed into this creative space where we can relive our cherished moments together. Through this website, we aim to share a piece of our journey — from unforgettable memories to snapshots of our university life. You'll find a collection of photos, stories, and experiences that capture the essence of our time together. In addition to personal memories, Rishikesan.Tech also offers a selection of eBooks that we believe will be valuable to students and anyone eager to learn. Whether you're here to relive our memories, find useful resources, or simply connect with a community of like-minded individuals, we hope you find something meaningful.

Thank you for visiting our website, and we invite you to join us in celebrating the spirit of friendship, learning, and growth!
